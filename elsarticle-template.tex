In this \replaced[id=kg]{study}{paper}, we present \name, an open-source and WebGL-based JavaScript library that supports \added[id=kg]{the }fast visualization of large-scale graph data (up to 50 thousand nodes and 1 million edges) at an interactive frame rate with a commodity computer. Experimental results demonstrate that our library outperforms existing toolkits (Sigma.js, D3.js, Cytoscape.js, and Stardust) \replaced[id=kg]{in}{on} both \deleted[id=kg]{the} performance and memory consumption.
